Phil's favorite movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

#1 The Princess Bride

Quite possibly the greatest movie of all time. It has everything. Fencing. Fighting. Torture. Poison. True love. Hate. Revenge. Giants. Hunters. Bad men. Good men. Beautifulest ladies. Snakes. Spiders. Beasts of all natures and descriptions. Pain. Death. Brave men. Coward men. Strongest men. Chases. Escapes. Lies. Truths. Passion. Miracles. Who could ask for more?

#2 The Starwars Saga

Another masterpiece of story and film. Lucas creates the first fully fledged space oddessy.

#3 So I Married an Axe Murderer

Possibly Mike Meyers best film ever. It's a laugh a minute of relatable comedy.
